3 Important Choices When Creating Your App

I have been dealing with a client recently that started their app project with great gusto and a ‘it needs to be done yesterday’ attitude that is very common. I was to build the UI for them using thier existing app as a base - now that we have gone passed thier imposed deadline of having the app ‘done’. I gave them a call to see how it was all going - well it seemed to be comming un-stuck at the seams!

So what questions should they have thought while they built thier app?

  1. Who is your app for?

  2. What platforms will you target?

  3. Will it continue?
