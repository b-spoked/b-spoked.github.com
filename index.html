Welcome

B-Spoked is all about custom web and mobile apps that are designed to enhance the users experience rather than distract them from thier lives.

The work/coding/engineering/hacking is done with pride and enthusiasim by Jamie who lives in Nelson NZ with his girls (a wife and two daughters). Jamie understands and loves code - he has been in the coding game for over 10 years working on projects as diverse as software for lawyers to apps for sports team supporters and communication platforms for local government agencies. So lots of good stuff has been done :)

Our Kind of Projects

Small projects with a clear focus is what we look for. Working towards and goal and delivering to that goal and helping you create apps that are appropriate to your needs and meet our ethos of ‘enhance don’t distract’.

Our Kind of Code

Keeping things simple and appropraite is where we are at.

The temptaion can be to over estimate or complicate what is actually required for your app - we prefer to look at the minimum requirements to make it functional and then work form there. We use best of breed technologies and approach’s to ensure this simplicity.
