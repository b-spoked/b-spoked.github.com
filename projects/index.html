What We Wanna

Our mission is to provide people with young kids a simple way to find suitable places to take them and discover the world of awesome that is around them.

What is What We Wanna?

A web application that works across devices so you can browse and then save place in your area that you and the kids would like to go. It could be a park, a walk, the beach, or maybe even just the closest toilets becuse the kids are busting.

Why would I use it?

  • You are a caregiver with young kids that you wants to get out of the house and discover all your area has to offer.
  • You are new to an area, or traveling, and don’t know whats on offer.
  • You know some great places that you wish to share with others so they can also enjoy them.

What is the road-map?

The road-map will largely be determined by feedback from users. The initial plans are to expand the offering with native phone applications and also the ability to plan your day out.

How will the app be sustained?

The aim is to continue to grow the app and our offering so as such the aim is to generate some form of revenue from the get go. At this stage it will be by offering relevant business the ability to advertise to the users of the app - i.e. kids and their caregivers.
