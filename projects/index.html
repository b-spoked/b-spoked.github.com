I guess like may parents I am always keen to find new places, and rediscover some old ones, to take the kids for some gool old fun. Returning home to my childhood home town with kids I realised I didn’t know where I could take them for some fun. Working purely for my own selfish goal.

Aims of What We Wanna

  • Keep it simple: With a clean UI that gives you what you need. It wasn’t the idea to have users tied to the app but rather used as a quick reference.

  • Easy to find details: We show you the notes in your area and how close they are to you.

  • Easy to add : If you find something new you are willing to share just add it.
